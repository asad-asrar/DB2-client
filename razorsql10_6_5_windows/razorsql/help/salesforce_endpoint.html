Salesforce Environment

Users can connect to the Salesforce production environment, the Salesforce sandbox environment, or a custom Salesforce URL. By default, RazorSQL connects to the production environment. To connect to a sandbox environment, change the value in the "Environment" drop down box to "Sandbox".

To connect to a custom URL, select the "Custom URL" option in the Environment drop-down. The URL should be in the following format (Note that RazorSQL uses the Salesforce SOAP API):

https://xxxxxxxxx.my.salesforce.com/services/Soap/u/52.0
